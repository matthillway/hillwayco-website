hillway.ai is the specialist technology and automation division of Hillway, created to empower commercial property professionals with powerful, intelligent tools built for the way surveyors actually work.

From automating time-consuming admin to building bespoke AI-powered platforms, we help landlords, property teams, and consultancies future-proof their operations through practical, applied innovation.

What Is hillway.ai?

hillway.ai is where property expertise meets intelligent automation. We are not just surveyors, we're PropTech developers, AI consultants, and automation architects. Our mission is simple:

To build tools that make your property business faster, smarter, and more profitable – without adding complexity.

Whether you're managing a regional portfolio or leading an institutional fund, our solutions help you unlock new efficiencies and insights from your property data.

What We Offer

01

Custom AI & Automation Tools for Surveyors

We build and deploy automation apps that streamline the most repetitive parts of the surveying and property management workflow. Example projects include:

  • Service Charge Budgeting Automations – Turn messy spreadsheets into clean, auditable outputs in seconds.
  • Lease Data Extraction Tools – Automatically extract and summarise key lease terms, dates, rents, and obligations from scanned PDFs.
  • Virtual Surveyor Assistants – AI tools that help surveyors complete inspections, condition reports, or site notes on the go.
  • Asset Reporting Dashboards – Link data across property systems to create live dashboards showing rent arrears, lease events, compliance, and value-add opportunities.

All built around your exact team workflows and always with an experienced Chartered Surveyor's insight behind the code.

02

App Development

Not every problem needs a huge software build. Our team codes and creates rapid prototypes and scalable internal apps, such as:

  • Property document libraries with search & version control
  • AI-powered invoice validation and Xero integration
  • Automated critical date reminders and escalation workflows
  • AI-powered assistants trained on your lease or service charge data

We build robust internal tools in days or weeks, not months, and integrate them directly with platforms you already use.

03

AI Strategy & Digital Transformation Consultancy

If you're not sure where to start, we offer AI and PropTech strategy support for business owners, surveyors, and property teams:

  • Process mapping and automation discovery
  • System audits and AI-readiness assessments
  • Platform selection and procurement guidance
  • AI strategy planning tailored to your portfolio and risk appetite
  • Team training and cultural change support to encourage adoption

We help you understand what AI can (and can't) do, then deliver measurable, low-risk improvements aligned to your goals.
