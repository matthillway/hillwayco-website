AutoTime Features for Construction Sites

Every AutoTime feature is designed to solve real problems faced by construction companies managing multiple sites and workers.

Geo-Fenced Site Boundaries

Set up virtual perimeters around your construction sites. AutoTime only allows workers to clock in when they're physically within the site boundary, eliminating false claims.

Photo ID Verification

AutoTime can require workers to take a selfie when clocking in, preventing buddy punching and ensuring the right person is on site. Photos are timestamped and GPS-tagged.

Real-Time Site Attendance

Site managers can see who's on-site right now through the AutoTime dashboard. Track late arrivals, early departures, and no-shows instantly.

Weekly Timesheet Reports

AutoTime automatically generates weekly timesheets showing hours worked per worker, per site. Download as PDF for records or Excel for further analysis.

Direct Xero Integration

Export approved timesheets from AutoTime straight to Xero for payroll. No manual data entry, no errors, just seamless payroll processing.

Offline Mode

AutoTime works even on remote sites without internet. Clock-ins sync automatically when connection is restored, ensuring no lost data.

Overtime & Break Tracking

AutoTime automatically calculates overtime based on your rules. Track breaks, including mandatory rest periods for compliance.

Multi-Site Management

Manage unlimited construction sites from one AutoTime account. Workers automatically clock into the correct site based on their GPS location.

Subcontractor Support

Track your own employees and subcontractor workers in AutoTime. Generate separate reports for different companies working on your sites.
