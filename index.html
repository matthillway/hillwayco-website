Founded on Experience

Hillway was founded by Matt Fitzgerald, a Chartered Surveyor whose 20+ year career spans the full spectrum of commercial property management across Sheffield, Doncaster and Yorkshire, from hands-on asset management to strategic portfolio restructuring for institutional investors. Matt's track record includes scaling property management operations from zero to over 4.75 million square feet, building high-performing teams that have successfully managed landmark assets throughout the UK for over a decade.

What sets Matt apart is his early recognition that traditional property management expertise needed to evolve through PropTech and automation. Since 2015, he has been pioneering the integration of cloud-based systems and building Tech-stack solutions and automations with traditional chartered surveyor practices, proving that PropTech and AI enhance rather than replace professional expertise. This philosophy has become Hillway's foundation: maintaining the rigorous standards that are expected of a RICS registered firm while leveraging PropTech automation to deliver efficiency gains that simply weren't possible with traditional methods alone.

We build long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and consistent delivery across Yorkshire, with dedicated teams in both Sheffield and Doncaster.

Our Services

At Hillway, we provide a comprehensive suite of commercial property consultancy services tailored to meet the diverse needs of landlords, investors, and in-house property teams. Each service is delivered with strategic insight, practical expertise, PropTech innovation, and a commitment to long-term value.

Property & Asset Management Consultancy

We offer full-spectrum commercial property and asset management for commercial portfolios, ensuring compliance, tenant satisfaction, and long-term asset value. Director-led oversight covers everything from service charge management to lease administration and maintenance coordination. Our flexible structure allows us to support portfolios of all sizes while maintaining the close, responsive service of a boutique consultancy enhanced by PropTech solutions.

Service Charge Consultancy & Advice

Expert guidance on service charge budgeting and administration, fully aligned with RICS standards. We ensure charges are managed correctly across across all properties, with all costs accurately allocated and fully recovered from tenants in line with lease terms and best practice. Transparent reporting enhanced by automation enhances tenant understanding and trust.

Digital Transformation & PropTech Integration

We assess your current systems and implement the right PropTech solutions, from cloud-based management platforms to AI-driven analytics, to automate routine tasks and turn data into actionable insights. Through Hillway.ai, we provide training and change management to ensure new PropTech is adopted smoothly, freeing your team from manual administration with smart workflows.

Facilities Management Advice

Strategic support to keep your properties safe, compliant, and operationally efficient. From health & safety compliance to planned maintenance programmes, we help ensure your sites are well-managed through risk management, service quality monitoring, and lifecycle planning enhanced by PropTech tools.

Lease Advisory

Clear, pragmatic advice on lease renewals, regears, rent reviews, and other matters for Yorkshire properties. Our MRICS-qualified experts negotiate favourable terms and resolve complex issues to enhance asset performance, informed by the latest market comparables and PropTech-driven trends analysis.

Strategic Property Management Advice

High-level consulting to help property owners structure effective in-house teams, streamline processes with PropTech, or reposition portfolios. We bring clarity through process audits, team development guidance, and portfolio strategy insights for long-term success using AI and automation.
